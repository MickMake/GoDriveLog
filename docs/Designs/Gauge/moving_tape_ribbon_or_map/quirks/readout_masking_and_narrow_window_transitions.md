# Readout masking and narrow-window transitions

**Gauge group:** [Moving tape, ribbon or map](<../README.md>)

## Definition

Partial or ambiguous readings caused by apertures that reveal only a small portion of a moving scale or drum.

This is a canonical umbrella label. The exact per-gauge wording and any qualifiers below remain the authoritative detail; gauges grouped here may reach the same visible symptom through different physics.
