# Multi-pointer and multi-channel interaction

**Gauge group:** [Radial pointer](<../README.md>)

## Definition

Visual or mechanical interaction among multiple pointers, scales or measurement channels.

This is a canonical umbrella label. The exact per-gauge wording and any qualifiers below remain the authoritative detail; gauges grouped here may reach the same visible symptom through different physics.

### [E25 — Concentric multi-pointer instrument](<../gauges/e25_concentric_multi_pointer_instrument.md>)

- **Measured or indicated:** Several related variables in one aperture
- **Era:** 1910s-present
- **Preserved source phrases:**
  - “Pointers cross, overlap and cast separate shadows”
  - “one movement may obscure another’s hub”
  - “failures can leave one hand frozen while others continue”
